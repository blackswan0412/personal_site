Throughline

I study the systems that shape how we connect, decide, and anticipate the future together. Today, I’m especially drawn to prediction markets and forecasting — mechanisms that turn individual uncertainty into collective clarity. They sit at the intersection of psychology, markets, and technology: the ultimate expression of distributed human intelligence.
